Welcome to Swanson

What is it?

Swanson is a web application that uses the Swanson API to generate quotes from the character Ron Swanson from Parks and Rec

How does it work?

Getting 1 quote

If you want a randomly selected Ron Swanson quote, just press the Random Quote button

Getting multiple quotes

If you want a selection of random quotes, enter the amount you want in the number bar, and press the Random Quotes button. You can cycle through the returned quotes with the Prev and Next buttons. If you want to see all of the returned quotes at once, you can press the All/Hide button to toggle the results.

Searching for quotes

Got a particular quote you wanna look for? Or maybe you just want know how many times Ron says something about bacon? Just enter the term you want to look for in the searchbar and press the Search Quotes button
